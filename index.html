Story 1. 듣고 읽기

Birds' Eyes

People can move their eyes. We can look left or right without moving our heads. But many birds can't move their eyes very much. They do not have a lot of muscles around their eyes.

Why not? Muscles are heavy. Birds must be light to fly. A bird with a lot of muscles can't fly very well. So birds don't have many muscles.

Owls do not have any eye muscles. They can't move their eyes at all. But owls can move their heads. In fact, they can turn their heads all the way around. They can look around easily without moving their eyes at all.

Owls can't move their eyes, but their eyes are very good in other ways. Owls' eyes are really big. They can see small animals very far away. Owls can also see well in the dark.

Story 2. 듣고 읽기

The Ship of the Desert

Camels live in the desert, and there are many interesting facts about camels. Some of them have two humps on their back, but others have one hump on their back.

The hump is full of fat. Camels don't have to eat very often. They can use the fat in their humps for energy. Then the hump becomes smaller.

Ships carry things across water. Camels carry things across sand. People call camels "ships of the desert." Camels can carry a lot of heavy things. They can carry as much as 455 kilograms. Camels also can drink about 41 liters of water in ten minutes.

Camels are popular in the Middle East. People use camel hair to make rugs, tents, and clothes. They even use camel dung to make fires for cooking. They also eat camels. People say camels taste like beef. Camels are useful in many different ways.
